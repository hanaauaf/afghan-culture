City Gallery

In this page you can know more about other cultural cities of Afghanistan.

Kabul-Capital City

Kabul is the cultural and political center of Afghanistan.

Mazar-e-Shaif-Blue Mosque

Mazar hosts the vibrant New Year festival at the Blue Mosque.

Badakhshan

Badakhshan is known for its stunning mountains and rare Lapis Lazuli stones.

Kandahar-Traditional and Historic

Kandahar is known as the founding city of modern Afghanistan.

Ghazni-Full of Islamic Architecture

Ghazni was once a powerful center of Islamic art and science.

Panjshir-Famous for its scenic beauty

Panjshir Valley is known for its natural beauty and heroic resistance.
